Change is News

We believe change itself is news. While Polymarket treats news as change, we reverse the perspective: prediction changes ARE the news.

🎯 Our Approach

Traditional news reports on events. We track prediction market movements and identify significant probability shifts as newsworthy events. When probability crosses key thresholds (30% / 70%), we generate news.

📊 Three Event Categories

  • 🆕 New High-Probability: Events that just crossed 70%
  • 🚪 Exited High-Probability: Events that fell below 70%
  • 🏆 Long-Term High-Probability: Stable events (5+ days)

🔮 Future Vision

Starting with technology events, we plan to expand to economy, geopolitics, and more. Our unique "change as news" perspective offers a fresh take on prediction market analysis.
